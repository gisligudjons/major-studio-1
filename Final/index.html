Wigs & Portraits

A look at the use of wigs in portraits throughout the centuries

The spiral displays the time axis. The axis starts in the 1660s and every bar represents a new decade. Every century is also distinguished by their color. The data is from two institutions of the Smithsonian, the National Portrait Gallery, and the Catalog of American Portraits.

The visualization is interactive! Hover your mouse over the bar to see the valur and the corresponding decade.
